AusPac Insurance Jobs v1 requirements

Core scope:
- Jobs should track insurer work from intake to completion.
- Activity records and rate lines should support costing and operational progress.
- Relationships should cover insurers, customers, installers, and scheduling contacts.
- Quote and proposal checkpoints should be visible before jobs are confirmed.
- Completion reporting and invoice tracking should be included in the operating model.
- Useful dashboards should surface work in progress, scheduling pressure, and completion throughput.

Implementation note:
- Treat this as an OCTO workspace design brief rather than a request for external integrations.
